Uses Glossary Use Cases

If you are wondering how to create a web site for business on a zero budget, Bitrix24.Sites is what you need. Unlike other multipurpose site
builders that let you create blogs, news sites, image galleries or forums, our solution only creates business sites.

This means that each website created with Bitrix24 comes with a form builder and a live chat widget. Information entered is automatically
captured inside free Bitrix24 CRM and can be later used with our free email marketing tools. We understand that the primary purpose of a
business website is to generate leads and sales, so our sites and landing pages are built around the idea that they are just an initial step of
the sales process. The leads can be then assigned to your sales agents, who can sell proactively or activate lead nurturing workflows.

Bitrix24 also supports quotes, invoices and online payments (PayPal, Stripe, BrainTree). Tasks, projects, calendars and collaboration are
available to all free Bitrix24.Sites users as well.
